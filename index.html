The future

We all use computers, we all use smartphones - wouldn't it be bad if it turned out that we're not in control of them?

Wouldn't it be bad if it turned out all of them require destroying the planet in order to be made?

It would be bad

So I think we should produce electronics that are built with ethically sourced materials, and ones that you can repair by yourself instead of throwing away.

And I believe that I you wanted to check what this program you installed does, like r e a l l y and not just in it's interface, you should be allowed to read it's code. Would you give your unlocked computer to a random employee of a company you're buying something from? No? So why do you do it, remotely?

I really think that computers are the future. I mean, they're already the present, but they will only become more important, not less, in the future. So it is absolutely imperative that they 1. don't destroy the planet and 2. don't give godlike powers to one or two people.

So what do we do?

Well I restore my old devices with FOSS software, and I want to ditch the cloud in favour of my own private server. But that is not nearly enough on a societal scale. Let's campaign for repairability, for business accountability, and for open source, together.

That's me!
